About

Recognition runs Swin-Food101, MobileCLIP-S2 and SlimSAM in your browser via ONNX Runtime Web. Nutrition data: USDA FoodData Central (FNDDS 2024-10-31). Packaged products: Open Food Facts (ODbL). Exercise energy: 2011 Compendium of Physical Activities with the ACSM formula. Estimates are informational, not medical advice.
